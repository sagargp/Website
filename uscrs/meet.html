Meet the team

 

The Underwater Robotics Team is split up into a handful of subteams. They are listed below.

Electrical Team

These guys take the whole concept of charge gradients to the next level. These guys manage to be creative, and yet grounded in their approach to our elecitrcal problems. Here's a list of things they do:
Power board
Gets power to the robot. Actually it does everything else too.
Wires
Superhighway of the charges.
Lights
SeaBee is not a cat.

Software Team

This is the best team. It's comprised of one man. Some say he naturally faces magnetic north, and he roams the woods at night foraging for wolves. All we know is, his commit messages are very terse.
Super code
Does everything.
ROS
This is like wires but in software.
Lights
Actually we code in the dark.

Mechanical Team

Without these guys, why would we bother to show up? They give us the inspiration to wake up in the morning, and the courage to go to sleep at night. If anything goes wrong, it's most likely their fault.
Floating
Sounds easy. Isn't.
Not falling apart
SeaBee is great at this, thanks to the mech. team.

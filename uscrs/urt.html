We went to the beach today.

Posted by Leah on 04/20/12 4:20:20 pm

It was amazingly windy. However we solved some problems with our dolphin unit. Turns out we ordered the wrong part. We got it sorted out and now the SeaBee flies underwater much like a penguin would. We had pizza, which was delicious. However a wind carried our pizza away and it got stuck in a tree. We ordered more but I often wonder if the first pizza is doing okay out there, on its own.

Dealing with things

Posted by SeaBee III on 12/26/11 04:36am

Well, another year came and went and I didn't get what I wanted for Christmas. People always get me stuff I don't really care about, like new battery pods and cameras. All I really want for Christmas are a nice pair of woolen, water resistant socks. It gets cold in that lab! I sit there all alone for days. Nobody comes in just to say 'hi' any more. It's always "Oh we need some new UDEV rules" or "Hey let's see if SeaBee can even power on?" Sometimes I wonder if it's even worth it.
